About Us

What is the Triend Travel App?

Let’s break this down. Triend is a user-led content sharing platform for like minded people who value real opinions of other travellers’ experiences around the world. It’s for the next generation that want to discover, personalise and experience travel in a new way post Covid-19. It’s for the travellers that want to do things differently, who want to help and support each other, so we can all keep sustainably travelling in this new world, post pandemic.

Get started

Why join the Triend Community?

You understand there is a need for travel to change. You love to travel globally or discover travel from your couch. You love community and get inspired by like minded people and trend setters. You believe in discovering new experiences, different from the normal tourist traps. You believe in ‘circular travel’. A concept where we can all help each other keep travelling and create a new sustainable future. If you answered yes to any of the above, we should be friends and you need to download the triend app now!

What We Do

App Features

Think Instagram meets Depop. Triend is half content platform, half travel shop. Whether you want to find travel inspiration and connect with real travellers or book your next getaway, Triend allows you to discover experiences you have never even heard of, all through trusted users.

Upload Your Triend

By uploading images and videos to create your favourite trips, others will not only be inspired by your trips but also given the opportunity to purchase your personalised experiences.

Personalisation

We are all different in this world. Here at Triend we know this. Our app will learn your travel loves and recommend where and what you should experience on your next journey suited to your interests.

Discover

Whether you want to find new experiences, be it a wellness retreat, staycation or an off the beat adventure trail. Triend has the hottest travel platform in town which will let you discover other travellers experiences.

Earn

Create your own marketplace and travel shop. Let others see what activities you did, where you stayed and what you experienced. If they book your trip, you make money. Simple as that!

Rate your Triender

Rate other Trienders on their trips and experiences. Were they really helpful in planning your Yoga Retreat in Sri Lanka, Music Festival in Iceland or Runcation in the UK? Let them know and build their trusted community.

Book your trip

Save hours of website searching with untrusted businesses. Book all your required travel in one place. With the confidence of authentic recommendations. From your flights and accommodation to experiences and events. Triend really is your one-stop travel marketplace.
